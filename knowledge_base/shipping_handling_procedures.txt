SHIPPING & HANDLING PROCEDURES

SHIPPING METHODS
================

Standard Shipping (2-5 Business Days)
- Cost: $15.99 for orders under $150
- Free shipping on orders over $150
- Uses FedEx Ground with temperature control
- Available to continental US only
- Not recommended for fresh products

Express Shipping (1-2 Business Days)
- Cost: $29.99 flat rate regardless of order size
- FedEx Priority Overnight or 2-Day
- Includes dry ice and insulated packaging
- Required for all fresh/unfrozen products
- Available nationwide including Alaska and Hawaii

Same-Day Delivery
- Available in select metropolitan areas
- Cost: $49.99 for orders under $200, $24.99 for orders over $200
- Available Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM delivery window
- Fresh products only
- Service areas: NYC, LA, Chicago, Dallas, Miami

PACKAGING SPECIFICATIONS
========================

Frozen Products
- Vacuum-sealed in commercial-grade packaging
- Placed in insulated foam coolers
- Dry ice quantities calculated for transit time
- Temperature monitoring devices included
- Arrives frozen solid (0°F or below)

Fresh Products
- Packaged in food-grade containers
- Refrigerated gel packs maintain 32-38°F
- Express shipping mandatory
- Must be received within 48 hours of shipping
- Signature required upon delivery

SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS
=====================

Geographic Restrictions
- Cannot ship to PO Boxes
- Military APO/FPO addresses: Contact customer service
- Alaska/Hawaii: Express shipping only, additional $25 surcharge
- International: Currently not available

Product Restrictions
- No shipping during extreme weather (above 90°F in destination)
- Holiday restrictions: No shipping Dec 23-26, Jan 1
- Some products restricted by state regulations (see compliance guide)

DELIVERY REQUIREMENTS
=====================

Customer Availability
- Signature required for all orders over $100
- Adult signature (21+) required for all orders
- Alternative delivery options: Hold at FedEx location
- Delivery attempts: Up to 3 attempts, then returned

Temperature Requirements
- Customer must refrigerate/freeze immediately upon receipt
- Products may be slightly thawed but still cold (acceptable)
- Completely thawed or warm products: Contact customer service immediately
- 4-hour window from delivery to refrigeration maximum

TRACKING & COMMUNICATION
========================

Order Confirmation
- Email sent within 1 hour of order placement
- Includes estimated ship date and tracking information
- Processing time: 1-2 business days for in-stock items

Shipping Notifications
- Email with tracking number sent when order ships
- SMS notifications available upon request
- Delivery day text alerts included
- Customer can reschedule delivery through tracking portal

PROBLEM RESOLUTION
==================

Delayed Shipments
- Automatic credit for shipping charges if delivery exceeds promised time
- Express replacement shipping for spoiled products
- Full refund option if customer cannot accept delayed delivery

Damaged or Compromised Products
- Photo documentation required for claims
- Replacement product shipped at no charge
- Credit issued for shipping costs
- Claims must be reported within 24 hours of delivery

Weather-Related Delays
- Shipments held until safe shipping conditions
- Customer notified of delays via email/SMS
- No additional charges for weather holds
- Expedited shipping when weather clears

SPECIAL HANDLING SERVICES
=========================

Gift Orders
- Special gift packaging available (+$9.99)
- Gift messages included at no charge
- Delivery coordination for special dates
- Gift receipt option (no pricing information)

Commercial Accounts
- Scheduled delivery options available
- Invoice payment terms (Net 30 with approval)
- Bulk shipping discounts for large orders
- Dedicated account representative

Custom Processing
- Additional processing time: 5-10 business days
- Special packaging requirements accommodated
- Temperature control throughout processing
- Custom labeling available for commercial accounts
