RETURNS & REFUNDS POLICY

GENERAL RETURN POLICY
=====================

Return Eligibility
- Products must be in original, unopened packaging
- Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery
- Products must have been properly stored (frozen/refrigerated)
- Custom processed items are not returnable
- Gift orders follow same return guidelines

Non-Returnable Items
- Opened or partially used products
- Products stored improperly or thawed
- Custom cuts or special processing orders
- Items past expiration date
- Products showing signs of tampering

Return Process
- Contact customer service within 30 days
- Return authorization (RA) number required
- Customer responsible for return shipping costs
- Original packaging required for return
- Inspection upon receipt determines refund amount

QUALITY GUARANTEE
=================

Our Quality Promise
- 100% satisfaction guarantee on product quality
- Products arrive fresh/frozen as ordered
- Proper temperature maintenance during shipping
- Products free from damage or contamination
- Accurate product description and weight

Quality Issues Coverage
- Products arriving thawed or warm
- Damaged packaging or compromised vacuum seals
- Off odors, colors, or textures indicating spoilage
- Incorrect products or quantities shipped
- Products past stated expiration dates

Reporting Quality Issues
- Contact customer service immediately upon receipt
- Photo documentation may be requested
- Keep products refrigerated pending resolution
- Do not consume products suspected of quality issues
- Report within 24 hours of delivery

REFUND PROCESSING
=================

Refund Methods
- Original payment method preferred
- Credit card refunds: 3-5 business days
- PayPal refunds: 1-2 business days
- Store credit option for faster processing
- Check refunds: 7-10 business days (if original payment unavailable)

Refund Amounts
- Full refund for quality issues
- Full refund for shipping errors
- Partial refunds for minor issues resolved with customer
- Return shipping costs refunded for company errors
- Processing fees non-refundable for customer-initiated returns

Refund Timeline
- Quality issues: Immediate processing upon verification
- Standard returns: 3-5 days after inspection
- Shipping error refunds: Same day processing
- Damaged goods: Next business day processing
- Email notification sent when refund is processed

REPLACEMENT OPTIONS
===================

Product Replacement
- Identical product replacement preferred
- Substitute products for discontinued items
- Upgraded products at no additional charge when appropriate
- Express shipping for replacement orders
- No charge for shipping on replacement orders

Replacement Process
- Faster than refund processing
- Ships within 24 hours for quality issues
- Original product disposal not required for quality issues
- Customer keeps defective product for quality claims
- Replacement tracking information provided

SHIPPING ISSUES
===============

Delayed Shipments
- Full shipping refund for delays beyond promised delivery
- Option to cancel order if delay is unacceptable
- Expedited shipping for replacement orders
- Communication throughout delay resolution
- Weather delays beyond our control may not qualify for refunds

Lost or Stolen Packages
- Investigation with shipping carrier required
- Replacement or refund after carrier investigation
- Signature confirmation helps prevent theft claims
- Security camera footage may be requested
- File police report for stolen packages over $200

Damaged Shipments
- Carrier damage claims filed on customer's behalf
- Immediate replacement shipped
- Customer not responsible for carrier damage
- Photo documentation of damage required
- Keep all packaging materials for carrier inspection

SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES
====================

Allergic Reactions
- Immediate medical attention encouraged
- Product lot information collected for investigation
- Full refund and medical expense consideration
- Product testing conducted if necessary
- Communication with quality assurance team

Product Recalls
- Automatic refund processing for recalled products
- Customer notification via email and phone
- Express shipping for replacement products
- No return shipping required for recalled items
- Disposal instructions provided

Power Outages/Equipment Failure
- Consideration for product loss due to customer equipment failure
- Case-by-case evaluation for refund/replacement
- Documentation of power outage may be required
- Partial credit may be available
- Insurance claim assistance if applicable

COMMERCIAL ACCOUNT RETURNS
==========================

Restaurant/Food Service Returns
- Extended 45-day return window
- Volume return considerations
- Replacement priority for business operations
- Invoice credit options available
- Dedicated commercial account support

Wholesale Returns
- Consignment return options for large orders
- Quality issue resolution within 4 hours
- Business-to-business return shipping arrangements
- Net 30 account credit processing
- Inventory replacement guarantees

GIFT ORDER RETURNS
==================

Gift Return Process
- Recipient can initiate returns directly
- Gift receipt option hides pricing information
- Store credit issued unless gift giver requests refund
- Extended holiday return period (January 31st)
- Gift message removed from return communications

Gift Card Refunds
- Gift cards purchased with credit card are refundable
- Gift cards received as gifts are not refundable for cash
- Store credit maintained for gift card value
- No expiration on gift card store credit
- Transfer of gift card value to new recipient possible

DISPUTE RESOLUTION
==================

Credit Card Chargebacks
- Respond to chargeback requests within 5 business days
- Provide shipping and delivery documentation
- Customer communication history included
- Product quality documentation provided
- Resolution sought directly with customer when possible

Customer Escalation
- Supervisor review for unresolved issues
- Manager authorization for exceptions to policy
- Customer satisfaction prioritized over strict policy adherence
- Documentation of all escalation decisions
- Follow-up to ensure customer satisfaction

Quality Control

- Packaging integrity verification
- Expiration date tracking systems
- Customer feedback integration into quality processes

Customer Education

- Realistic expectation setting for exotic products
