ORDERING PROCESS GUIDE

ACCOUNT SETUP
=============

Creating an Account
- Visit www.exoticmeats.com/register
- Required information: Name, email, phone, billing address
- Age verification required (must be 18+)
- Email confirmation within 5 minutes
- Account activation immediate after verification

Account Types
- Individual/Retail: Standard consumer pricing
- Restaurant/Commercial: Volume discounts, special terms
- Wholesale: Requires business license, minimum orders
- Gift Account: For gift orders only, no stored payment methods

LOGIN ASSISTANCE
- Password reset: Automated email link
- Account lockout: After 5 failed attempts, 24-hour lockout
- Two-factor authentication available for enhanced security

PRODUCT SELECTION
=================

Browsing Products
- Categories: Game Meats, Poultry, Specialty Meats, Processed Items
- Filter options: Price range, cut type, quantity, availability
- Search function: Name, species, or description keywords
- Product comparison tool available
- Nutritional information provided for all products

Product Information
- Detailed descriptions and cooking recommendations
- High-resolution product images
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Nutritional facts and allergen information
- Source farm information and certifications

Quantity Selection
- Individual portions available
- Bulk pricing for quantities over 5 lbs
- Family packs (serves 4-6 people)
- Commercial quantities (25+ lbs)
- Custom quantities available upon request

SHOPPING CART
=============

Adding Items

- Bulk add feature for large orders

Cart Management
- Edit quantities before checkout
- Remove items or save for later
- Apply promo codes and discounts
- View shipping estimates
- Express checkout for saved payment methods

Price Calculations
- Real-time pricing updates
- Volume discounts automatically applied
- Shipping calculator based on zip code
- Tax calculations for applicable states
- Final total shown before payment

================

Shipping Information

Payment Methods
- Credit cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover

- Business accounts: Net 30 terms available

Order Verification
