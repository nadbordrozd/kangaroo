EXOTIC MEATS PRODUCT CATALOG

GAME MEATS
==========

Venison (Deer)
- Farm-raised red deer from New Zealand
- Rich, lean meat with earthy flavor
- Available cuts: steaks, roasts, ground meat, sausages
- Price range: $25-45 per pound
- Cooking recommendation: Medium-rare to medium

Wild Boar
- European wild boar, farm-raised
- Bold, slightly sweet flavor with tender texture
- Available cuts: chops, tenderloin, shoulder roasts, bacon
- Price range: $20-35 per pound
- Cooking recommendation: Cook thoroughly, pairs well with fruit

Elk
- Rocky Mountain elk, sustainably sourced
- Lean, mild flavor similar to beef but richer
- Available cuts: steaks, roasts, ground meat
- Price range: $30-50 per pound
- Cooking recommendation: Do not overcook, best at medium-rare

Bison/Buffalo
- American bison, grass-fed and free-range
- Sweet, rich flavor, leaner than beef
- Available cuts: steaks, burgers, roasts, jerky
- Price range: $18-40 per pound
- Cooking recommendation: Cook 25% less time than beef

EXOTIC POULTRY
==============

Quail
- Farm-raised Coturnix quail
- Delicate, slightly gamey flavor
- Sold whole or semi-boneless
- Price: $8-12 per bird (4-6 oz each)
- Cooking recommendation: Quick cooking, don't overcook

Duck
- Pekin and Muscovy varieties
- Rich, fatty meat with robust flavor
- Available: whole duck, legs, breasts, confit
- Price range: $12-25 per pound
- Cooking recommendation: Score skin, render fat slowly

Pheasant
- Ring-necked pheasant, farm-raised
- Lean white meat, mild gamey flavor
- Available whole or in parts
- Price: $20-30 per bird
- Cooking recommendation: Brine before cooking to prevent dryness

Ostrich
- Red meat alternative, very lean
- Beef-like flavor and texture
- Available cuts: steaks, fillets, ground meat
- Price range: $25-40 per pound
- Cooking recommendation: Serve rare to medium-rare

SPECIALTY MEATS
===============

Kangaroo
- Australian sourced, wild-caught
- Extremely lean, strong flavor
- Available as steaks and ground meat
- Price range: $35-50 per pound
- Cooking recommendation: Sear quickly, serve rare

Rabbit
- Domestic rabbit, humanely raised
- Mild, lean white meat
- Available whole or cut up
- Price: $15-20 per pound
- Cooking recommendation: Braise or roast, similar to chicken

Alligator
- Farm-raised American alligator
- White meat, mild fish-like flavor
- Available as tail meat, ribs, and steaks
- Price range: $20-30 per pound
- Cooking recommendation: Don't overcook, fry or grill

Yak
- Tibetan yak, high-altitude raised
- Rich, sweet flavor, very lean
- Available cuts: steaks, roasts, ground meat
- Price range: $40-60 per pound
- Cooking recommendation: Similar to beef but cook less
