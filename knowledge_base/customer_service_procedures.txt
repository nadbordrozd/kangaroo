CUSTOMER SERVICE PROCEDURES

CALL HANDLING STANDARDS
=======================

Answering Procedures
- Answer within 3 rings maximum
- Greeting: "Thank you for calling Exotic Meats Company, this is [Name], how may I help you today?"
- Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- Listen actively and take notes during conversation
- Confirm customer information before proceeding

Hold Procedures
- Never place customer on hold without permission
- "May I place you on brief hold while I look that up?"
- Check back every 30-45 seconds if hold exceeds 1 minute
- Apologize for any extended hold times
- Offer callback option for holds exceeding 3 minutes

Transfer Procedures
- Explain why transfer is necessary
- Provide extension number to customer
- Brief receiving department before transfer
- Stay on line to ensure successful connection
- Follow up if transfer fails

CUSTOMER IDENTIFICATION
======================

Account Lookup Process
- Verify customer identity using phone number or email
- Confirm billing address for additional verification
- For order inquiries, verify order number and billing zip code
- For new customers, explain account benefits
- Document any contact preference updates

Privacy Protection
- Never discuss account details without proper verification
- Protect customer personal information
- Follow CCPA and GDPR privacy guidelines
- Document consent for marketing communications
- Respect opt-out requests immediately

Information Updates
- Verify current contact information at call start
- Update address changes immediately in system
- Confirm email preferences for communications
- Document dietary restrictions or allergies
- Note special delivery instructions

ORDER MANAGEMENT
================

Order Status Inquiries
- Locate order using order number or customer information
- Provide current status (processing, shipped, delivered)
- Give tracking information if order has shipped
- Explain any delays and expected resolution
- Offer expedited shipping for time-sensitive orders

Order Modifications
- Verify modification is possible based on order status
- Explain any additional costs for changes
- Process approved modifications immediately
- Send confirmation email for all changes
- Document modification reason in order notes

Order Cancellations
- Confirm cancellation request and reason
- Process if order hasn't shipped
- Explain refund timeline and method
- Offer store credit for faster processing
- Document cancellation reason for trend analysis

Rush Orders
- Verify product availability for expedited processing
- Explain rush order fees and timeline
- Confirm customer acceptance of additional costs
- Flag order for priority processing
- Follow up to ensure on-time delivery

PRODUCT SUPPORT
===============

Product Information Requests
- Use product knowledge base for accurate information
- Provide cooking instructions and temperature guidelines
- Explain storage and handling requirements
- Discuss flavor profiles and cooking recommendations
- Offer recipe suggestions when appropriate

Cooking Assistance
- Transfer complex cooking questions to culinary team
- Provide basic cooking temperature and time guidelines
- Recommend cooking methods for specific cuts
- Explain differences between exotic and conventional meats
- Schedule cooking consultation for large orders

Nutritional Information
- Provide available nutritional data for products
- Explain health benefits of exotic meats
- Discuss dietary compatibility (keto, paleo, etc.)
- Refer to packaging labels for allergen information
- Connect with nutrition specialist for detailed questions

PROBLEM RESOLUTION
==================

Quality Complaints
- Listen without interrupting and express empathy
- Gather detailed information about the issue
- Document complaint with photos if available
- Offer immediate resolution (replacement/refund)
- Escalate to quality assurance team
- Follow up within 24 hours

Shipping Issues
- Verify delivery status and tracking information
- Contact shipping carrier for investigation if needed
- Offer replacement shipping for damaged packages
- Process shipping refunds for late deliveries
- Arrange redelivery for missed deliveries

Billing Disputes
- Review billing details with customer
- Explain charges and provide invoice breakdown
- Process authorized refunds or credits
- Escalate complex billing issues to accounting
- Document dispute resolution for future reference

Returns Processing
- Verify return eligibility based on policy
- Issue return authorization (RA) number
- Provide return shipping instructions
- Process refund upon receipt and inspection
- Offer store credit option for faster processing

ESCALATION PROCEDURES
====================

Supervisor Escalation
- Recognize when supervisor intervention is needed
- Brief supervisor on situation before transfer
- Remain available to assist if needed
- Document escalation reason and resolution
- Follow up with customer after supervisor contact

Department Transfers
- Quality issues: Quality Assurance (ext. 311)
- Commercial accounts: Commercial team (ext. 201)
- Technical problems: IT Support (ext. 411)
- Food safety concerns: Food Safety (ext. 311)
- Billing issues: Accounting (ext. 203)

Manager Authorization Required
- Refunds over $500
- Policy exceptions
- Freight claims over $200
- Commercial pricing adjustments
- Complaint settlements over $1000

CUSTOMER COMMUNICATION
======================

Follow-up Procedures
- Follow up on all quality complaints within 24 hours
- Check on delivery for expedited orders
- Confirm satisfaction after problem resolution
- Schedule follow-up calls for complex issues
- Document all follow-up activities

Email Communication
- Use company email templates for consistency
- Include order numbers and relevant details
- Copy customer service manager on complaints
- Respond to emails within 2 hours during business hours
- Maintain professional tone in all communications

Documentation Requirements
- Record all customer interactions in CRM system
- Note customer preferences and special requirements
- Document complaint details and resolutions
- Track order modifications and reasons
- Maintain call logs for quality assurance

SPECIAL SITUATIONS
==================

Emergency Procedures
- Food safety emergencies: Immediate transfer to ext. 311
- Medical emergencies related to products: Document details, escalate immediately
- Product recall situations: Follow recall protocol checklist
- After-hours emergencies: Contact emergency line ext. 911

VIP Customer Handling
- Identify VIP status in customer record
- Provide priority service and expedited processing
- Assign dedicated representative when possible
- Offer special accommodations within policy
- Report VIP interactions to customer service manager

International Customers
- Explain current shipping limitations
- Collect information for future expansion notification
- Provide timeline for international service availability
- Suggest alternative products available domestically
- Document international demand for planning purposes

Commercial Account Support
- Transfer to commercial team for pricing inquiries
- Verify business credentials for account setup
- Explain commercial terms and benefits
- Schedule meetings with account representatives
- Process purchase orders and invoicing requests
