TEMPERATURE CONTROL STANDARDS
=============================

Cold Chain Management
- Products maintained at 0°F during frozen storage
- Fresh products kept at 32-38°F throughout distribution
- Loading dock temperatures monitored continuously
- Transport vehicles equipped with temperature recorders
- Customer delivery verification required

Critical Temperature Points
- Receiving: Must be 0°F or below for frozen, 38°F or below for fresh
- Storage: Frozen at -10°F, fresh at 32-35°F
- Processing: Maintained below 40°F during handling
- Packaging: Climate-controlled environment (35-40°F)
- Shipping: Dry ice maintains sub-zero temperatures

CUSTOMER SAFETY GUIDELINES
==========================

Safe Handling Instructions
- Wash hands before and after handling raw meat
- Use separate cutting boards for raw meat
- Clean all surfaces with sanitizing solution
- Cook to recommended internal temperatures
- Refrigerate leftovers within 2 hours

Recommended Cooking Temperatures
- Ground meats: 160°F internal temperature
- Whole muscle meats: 145°F with 3-minute rest
- Poultry: 165°F internal temperature
- Game meats: 160°F (well-done recommended)
- Use calibrated meat thermometer for accuracy

Thawing Guidelines
- Refrigerator thawing: 24-48 hours depending on size
- Cold water thawing: Submerge in cold water, change every 30 minutes
- Microwave thawing: Cook immediately after thawing
- Never thaw at room temperature
- Once thawed, use within 1-2 days

STORAGE RECOMMENDATIONS
======================

Freezer Storage
- Maintain 0°F or below
- Use within recommended timeframes:
  * Ground meats: 3-4 months
  * Steaks/chops: 6-12 months
  * Whole animals: 12 months
  * Processed products: See package dating

Refrigerator Storage
- Maintain 40°F or below
- Store in original packaging or airtight containers
- Place on bottom shelf to prevent dripping
- Use fresh products within 1-3 days
- Check for spoilage signs before use
